release: v0.2.6

fix: disable gzip on SSE streams to fix alerts/log filters through ingress (#2)

Dozzle uses SSE (not WebSocket) for log streaming and alerts.
When gzip is advertised, Dozzle compresses the SSE stream, which
the HA Supervisor aiohttp proxy buffers instead of streaming.
Strip Accept-Encoding so SSE flows as plain text through the
entire proxy chain.
